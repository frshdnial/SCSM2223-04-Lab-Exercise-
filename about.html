Introduction

Assalamualaikum and hi everyone! I’m Farish Danial, but you can call me Farish. I’m originally from Ampang, Selangor, and I grew up in a family of five where I’m the oldest child among three siblings. Being the eldest in the family has taught me a lot about responsibility and leadership from a young age. Sometimes it means setting a good example for my younger siblings, helping them when they need advice, or simply being someone they can rely on. Growing up in a lively household with my parents and siblings has made my home environment fun and supportive, and it has helped shape me into a more patient and understanding person.

Before continuing my current studies, I completed my Foundation in Engineering at UiTM Dengkil. That experience strengthened my interest in technology and problem solving, which is one of the reasons why my ambition is to become a rich and successful software engineer in the future.

During my free time, I enjoy playing games, watching K-dramas, and scrolling through TikTok. These activities help me relax and recharge after a busy day.

When it comes to food, one of my all-time favorites is Ayam Gepuk, especially from Ayam Gepuk Top Global. I really enjoy the crispy chicken combined with the spicy sambal that gives it a strong and satisfying flavor. The spicy kick mixed with the crunchy texture makes every bite exciting, and it’s the kind of meal that instantly improves my mood when I’m hungry.

To go with that, my favorite drink is an iced matcha latte especially from ZUS Coffee. I like the unique taste of matcha because it has a slightly bitter yet creamy flavor when mixed with milk. When it’s served cold, it feels very refreshing and relaxing, especially after a long day.

For music, one of my favorite songs is Risk It All by Bruno Mars. I like how emotional and powerful the song feels. The lyrics and the way Bruno Mars sings them make the song sound passionate and inspiring, and it always reminds me that sometimes we need to take risks in order to achieve something meaningful in life.

A fun fact about me is that I love spending my holidays working part time at Habib Jewels. It’s not only about earning extra income, but also about meeting different types of people. Through that experience, I’ve learned how to communicate with many personalities and understand people better. I believe this helps me develop stronger emotional intelligence, which is an important skill in both personal life and future careers.
